Metadata template (Nunjucks) for rendering every synced Raindrop.io highlights & annotations.

Some notes:

  • The rendered result is placed in the front matter of the generated post.
  • If the rendered result does not follow the YAML syntax, the plugin will reject the update.
  • raindrop_id and raindrop_highlights properties are transparently added and updated by the plugin.
  • Available variables to use are the same as the previous template.
